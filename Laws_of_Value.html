LAWS OF VALUE

posted on August 13, 2018

A law is a controlling regulation and a rule of living conformable to righteousness. It is also an edict or decree. There are regulation that controls values. Spiritual laws are absolute and cannot be defiled.

Value is the property or aggregate of properties of a thing by which it is rendered useful or desirable. Value is worth and excellence. It is also the market price placed on a commodity.

The law of the producer: the value of a product is determined by the producer and not the end user. The messenger and the message should be the same to show the value of the messenger and the message ceases to be authentic if there’s a disparity between the messenger and the message Romans 2:24. The quality of a product reflects the quality of the producer. God placed laws so we can enjoy life if we obey the laws.

If you’re in Christ and your past is still hunting you then it is not God Romans 8;1. to understand the standard of a product you must understand the producer.Isaiah 46:9 The person of the producer(God): The producer knows what he put in the product (malachi 3:6) The one that produced you does not change, a product loses value if he is not in touch with the producer.

With God there’s no adjustment because his plans does not change, other producers may change their plans due to some defaults but god does not. God is faithful to what he says and true to who he is (Numbers 23:19 ‘God is not a man that he should lie, he is not human that he should change his mind.

Does he speak and then not act? Does he promise and not fulfill?’) Your manufacturer(God) cannot lie, he doesn’t have the ability to lie even if he does it is the truth. When God makes something it is a masterpiece so if God created you, you are a masterpiece, you are unique because you were made original by an original God.

you’re too important to God so don’t ever think for any second that you’re not valuable to him, that’s how much he loves you.Our value is not in material things, not in cars, shoes nor money. Our value is in Jesus, whatever social media is selling to you is not your worth. You are God’s most treasured possession and the apple of his eyes.

Yours in service

CeCe Ekwuagana
